## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

Is your dog scratching their ear more than usual? Is your cat squinting one eye? PetSnap Health Checker gives you trusted, vet-sourced answers in seconds — right from your phone.

HOW IT WORKS
Point your camera at your pet's symptom, or pick a body area and select what you see. PetSnap cross-references your input against the Merck Veterinary Manual, PetMD, and ASPCA databases to surface the most likely conditions, their severity, and what to do next — whether that's monitoring at home or heading to the vet.

WHAT YOU GET
• AI-powered symptom analysis for dogs, cats, and more
• Conditions ranked by likelihood with plain-English explanations
• Severity ratings (Mild / Moderate / Severe / Emergency)
• Home care steps and when-to-see-a-vet guidance
• Full health history for every pet — shareable with your vet
• Multi-pet support: dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, and more
• Dark mode, accessible design, works offline for history

TRUSTED SOURCES
Every result is backed by peer-reviewed veterinary literature from the Merck Veterinary Manual, PetMD, and ASPCA — the same references your vet uses.

FREE & PREMIUM
Free plan includes 3 symptom scans per month — enough to check on occasional concerns. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited scans, priority analysis, and full access to the Education Library with 100+ condition guides.

Premium: $4.99/month or $39.99/year (save 33%). Cancel anytime. No commitment.

IMPORTANT: PetSnap is an informational tool and does not replace professional veterinary care. In an emergency, contact your vet or an emergency animal hospital immediately.
