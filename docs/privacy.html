Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2026

PetSnap Journal is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Pet journal data: Details about your pet and personal observations that you choose to log. This data is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any server.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics to improve our services.
  • Purchase information: Subscription data processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment details directly.
  • Device information: Basic device and OS data for app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the App and deliver its features
  • To manage your subscription
  • To improve and maintain the App
  • To respond to support requests

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers (RevenueCat for subscription management) solely to operate the App. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations. No pet journal data is shared with any third party.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Pet journal data is stored exclusively on your device and is not accessible to us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will notify you of significant changes.
